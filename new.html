Pioneering Technology

Freevalve Technology
Pneumatic and electromagnetic actuators replace the conventional camshaft, granting individual control over each valve for unprecedented power delivery and efficiency.
Patented · KPS
Carbon Fiber Monocoque
Every panel, chassis component and structural element is crafted from aerospace-grade carbon fiber, achieving a body-in-white weight of just 72 kilograms.
Aerospace Grade
9-Speed LST Gearbox
The Light Speed Transmission can pre-select and execute any of 9 gear ratios simultaneously in under two milliseconds, eliminating torque interruption entirely.
Koenigsegg LST
Twin-Turbo V8 Engine
The 5.0L twin-turbocharged V8 produces 1,600hp on E85 fuel. Bespoke crankshaft, titanium connecting rods, and flat-plane design define its character.

Triplex Suspension
A third damper per axle controls chassis squat and pitch independently, maintaining optimal aerodynamic ride height at all speeds and under all load conditions.
